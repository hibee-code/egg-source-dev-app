Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

This privacy policy is designed in strict compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 as audited by the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

1. Introduction

Welcome to Egg Connect. Egg Connect ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal information when you access our mobile and web applications and use our egg sourcing, verification, and escrow protection services.

By registering an account, purchasing eggs, or listing poultry products on Egg Connect, you consent to the processing of your personal data as outlined in this policy.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We collect information that identifies or can be reasonably associated with you. This includes:

  • Identity & Account Details: Full name, email address, telephone number, user role (Buyer, Farm Owner, Administrator), and verification documents (such as government-issued IDs for farm owners).
  • Poultry Farm Profile Details: Farm name, state, local government area (LGA), physical address, inventory descriptions, production scale, and location coordinate metrics.
  • Transactional Records: Listing details, booking requests, transactional histories, invoice records, and escrow transaction states.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device model, operating system, browser configuration, and application interaction metrics.

3. Purposes for Processing Data

We process your personal data for clear, specific, and lawful purposes under Nigerian law, including:

  • Facilitating the connection between commercial egg buyers and verified poultry farms.
  • Operating and managing our secure booking and escrow protection transaction system.
  • Verifying the identity of farm operators to prevent fraudulent listings.
  • Sending critical transactional updates, invitation links, OTP notifications, and security alerts.
  • Complying with regulatory obligations under the Cybercrimes Act, FCCPA guidelines, and tax laws.

4. Legal Grounds for Processing

Under Section 25 of the NDPA 2023, we process your information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: You have given clear consent for us to process your data for specific operations.
  • Contract Fulfillment: Processing is necessary to execute the contract/order agreement between you (buyer or seller) and Egg Connect.
  • Legal Obligation: Processing is necessary to comply with financial, cyber-safety, or statutory audit requirements in Nigeria.
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing supports our service delivery, security audit trail logs, and optimization of user layouts.

5. Data Security and Safeguards

Egg Connect implements robust technical, organizational, and physical security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We utilize industry-standard TLS encryption for data in transit, secure database hashing algorithms (e.g. bcrypt for password storage), server-level audit trails, and strict role-based access controllers (RBAC).

6. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Pursuant to the NDPA 2023, you hold the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access: You can request confirmation of and access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request immediate correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal records.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request the deletion of your account and personal data, subject to regulatory transaction-retention mandates.
  • Right to Object/Restrict: You can object to the processing of your data for direct marketing or request restrictions on processing during dispute investigations.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request to receive your profile and listing details in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

7. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Your personal data is primary hosted on secure cloud infrastructure located within Nigeria or in jurisdictions that guarantee an adequate level of data protection in compliance with Section 41 of the NDPA 2023. Any international transfer of data complies with NDPC audited safeguard mechanisms.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, satisfy legal audit logs requirements, or resolve transactional escrow disputes. Upon expiration of the retention period, data is securely anonymized or permanently deleted.
